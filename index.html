Our Focus Areas

Energy Transition

The global energy infrastructure is undergoing the largest reallocation of capital in history. We identify investments across renewable energy infrastructure, energy storage, grid modernization, and the enabling technologies that power decarbonization.

Technology & Space

Transformational technology sectors—from satellite infrastructure to advanced computing to digital infrastructure—are reshaping economic value creation. We invest in the platforms, technologies, and companies building the next generation of connectivity and compute.

Strategic Materials

Advanced materials—graphene, rare earths, specialty alloys, and engineered compounds—are the substrate for every technological breakthrough. We identify materials innovations with high barriers to entry and significant value creation potential.

Investment Thesis

We look for opportunities at the intersection of:

  • Fundamental secular tailwinds
  • Regulatory or policy tailwinds
  • Addressable market expansion
  • Emerging competitive advantages
