# O'Dwyer Capital Brand Guidelines

## Overview
O'Dwyer Capital is a family investment office brand that conveys heritage, stability, growth, and timeless value. The brand identity combines Celtic heritage symbolism with modern financial sophistication.

---

## Logo System

### Primary Logo
- **File**: `logo-main.png` (2816x1536px)
- **Usage**: Master logo for all primary applications
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Spacing**: Maintain minimum clear space of 20px around logo

### Logo Variations

#### Favicon
- **File**: `favicon.png` (32x32px)
- **Usage**: Website browser tabs, bookmarks
- **Best for**: Digital applications requiring small icon

#### Small Logo
- **File**: `logo-small.png` (100x100px)
- **Usage**: Website headers, email signatures, document headers
- **Best for**: Navigation bars, menus, small placements

#### Medium Logo
- **File**: `logo-medium.png` (200x200px)
- **Usage**: Social media profiles, landing page hero sections
- **Best for**: Balanced, prominent placements

#### Large Logo
- **File**: `logo-large.png` (400x400px)
- **Usage**: Print materials, presentations, large displays
- **Best for**: Posters, large format applications

## Typography

### Font Recommendations
- **Headlines**: Segoe UI or Georgia (serif alternative)
- **Body Text**: Segoe UI or Open Sans
- **Logo Text**: Current Celtic + modern sans-serif combination

### Usage Guidelines
- Headlines: Bold, Dark Green (`#1a4d2e`)
- Body text: Regular weight, Neutral Gray (`#666666`)
- Tagline: Small caps, Gold accent (`#d4a574`)

## Logo Composition

The O'Dwyer Capital logo consists of three elements:

1. **Celtic Knot Symbol** (Top)
   - Gold color: `#d4a574`
   - Represents heritage and interconnected generations
   
2. **Upward Arrow** (Integrated into knot)
   - Represents growth and positive trajectory
   - Integrated seamlessly with Celtic design

3. **Text Elements**
   - "O'DWYER CAPITAL": Dark Green, primary branding
   - "INVESTED IN WHAT ENDURES": Tagline, smaller scale

---

## Logo Placement Guidelines

### Minimum Size
- **Print**: 1 inch (2.54cm) width minimum
- **Digital**: 100px width minimum
- **Favicon**: No minimum (use favicon.png)

### Clear Space
- Maintain at least 20px of clear space around the entire logo
- Never place other design elements within this clear space
- Exception: On solid color backgrounds, may reduce to 10px

### Background Usage
- **Preferred**: White or transparent backgrounds
- **Acceptable**: Dark Green backgrounds with white logo variant (if available)
- **Avoid**: Busy patterns, conflicting colors

### Spacing Rules
- Logo to left: navigation or content 30px minimum
- Logo top margin: 15-20px on web pages
- Logo bottom margin: 15-20px before content

## Application Examples

### Website Header
- Use Small Logo (100x100px)
- Position: Left side
- Background: White with subtle shadow

### Email Signature
- Use Small Logo (100x100px)
- Include company name and contact details below

### Business Cards
- Use Small Logo (100x100px)
- Top right or centered top
- Keep typography minimal

### Presentations
- Use Medium Logo (200x200px) on title slide
- Use Small Logo (100x100px) in header/footer
- Maintain 20px clear space

### Social Media
- Profile: Use Medium Logo (200x200px) cropped as needed
- Posts: Use Medium Logo centered or top-left
- Stories: Use Small Logo for branding

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✓ DO
- Use the provided logo files at appropriate sizes
- Maintain clear space around the logo
- Use official color palette
- Keep logo in its original proportions
- Use transparent PNG files for clean backgrounds

### ✗ DON'T
- Distort or stretch the logo
- Change colors without brand approval
- Place logo on conflicting backgrounds without proper contrast
- Rotate the logo at odd angles
- Add shadows, outlines, or effects not in original design
- Use outdated logo files or create new versions
